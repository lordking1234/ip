which factors show represent a good healthy network

  • Network Security:

    Look for crypto networks that have strong security measures in place. This includes features like two-factor authentication, encryption, and multi-signature transactions.

  • Network Activity:

    Look for networks with a high level of activity, such as high trading volume, new users, and active community participation.

  • Network Growth:

    Look for networks that are growing and expanding. This includes networks with new coins, new features, and new partnerships.

  • Network Legitimacy:

    Look for networks that are regulated or backed by reputable organizations or governments. This adds an extra layer of trust and legitimacy to the network.

  • Network Fees:

    Look for networks with low transaction fees. This helps ensure that users are not overpaying for their transactions.

Since the airdrop, the number of users, the number of transactions and the number of generated contracts on Aptos network have seen a decrease. However, in the first week of March, these metrics saw a slight increase, likely due to the increasing interest in the network, but have since decreased again. This could be attributed to the initial hype of the airdrop wearing off, as well as the current market conditions. The volume of transactions has also seen a decrease, with a gradual decline since the airdrop. This could be indicative of users taking a more conservative approach to their investments and transactions.

Additionally, this indicates that the network is used by users who are looking to capitalize on different fluctuations in the market, allowing them to quickly adjust their portfolio to maximize their profits.
